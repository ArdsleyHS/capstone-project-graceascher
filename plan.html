Steps to Success

  1. Come to the sessions prepared. Over the course of the program, there will be multiple homework sheets and/or problems that I will assign, and it is your job as the student to complete them on time nad thoughroughly.

  2. Be ready to participate. During the sessions, I will most likely not be the one talking the whole time. Your learning is a collabrative effort.

  3. See the glass half-full. If negative thoughts intrude your work space, there is no way you can learn as effectively. Come in with a positive attitude, and don't be put off by some tough questions!
