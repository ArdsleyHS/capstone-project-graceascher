What should I expect?


This is a common question before every parent commits their child or children to my program. I fully empathize with this sentiment - you do not know exactly what you're commiting yourself to. I am going to explain the basics of my process. The first session is free and I simply begin with meeting your child and assess their strengths and weaknesses. From here, I will ask a set of questions to futher understand, and develop a schedule that is tailored to their needs.
